Excellence in Higher Education

At ESSU Arteche Extension Campus, we offer programs that equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Our commitment is to provide accessible, quality higher education that shapes graduates to become competent, socially responsible, and globally competitive professionals.

Below are the bachelor’s degree programs offered at our campus:

Programs Offered

Bachelor’s degree programs available at ESSU AEC

Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd)

Designed to prepare future elementary school teachers with strong foundations in pedagogy, curriculum development, and classroom management.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd)

Equips aspiring high school teachers with specialized training in their chosen major fields, ensuring excellence in instruction and mentorship.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

Provides students with skills in programming, systems development, networking, and digital innovation for careers in the tech industry.

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

Trains students in hotel, tourism, and restaurant management, focusing on service excellence, leadership, and industry-standard practices.

Learning Philosophy

Our approach to teaching and learning

We believe in holistic education that balances knowledge, skills, and values. Students are engaged in academic instruction, research, and community service, ensuring they develop into well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of the modern world.

By combining classroom learning with hands-on experiences, we foster critical thinking, creativity, and a spirit of service to the community.
